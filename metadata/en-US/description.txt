Azkar is an app for reading and memorizing various duas and adhkar (morning, evening, and after-prayer).

ADHKAR & DUA
• Morning and evening adhkar (Hisn al-Muslim and Azkar.ru collections)
• After-prayer adhkar
• "100 Duas from the Quran and Sunnah"
• Audio recitation for correct pronunciation
• Two reading modes for convenient study with translation
• Repetition counter with auto-advance to the next dhikr
• Transliteration using DIN 31635 standard

APPEARANCE
• 6 color themes and dark mode
• 11 app icon options
• Multiple fonts for Arabic text and translation
• Adjustable text size
• Show/hide diacritical marks (tashkeel)

REMINDERS
• Notifications for morning and evening adhkar
• Friday (Jumu'ah) reminder
• Custom notification sounds

WIDGETS & INTEGRATION
• Home screen widgets
• Spotlight search
• Share dhikr as image or text
